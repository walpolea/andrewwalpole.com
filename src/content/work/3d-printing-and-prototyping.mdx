# 3D Printing and Prototyping

I've been experimenting, designing, prototyping and inventing with 3D printing since 2016. I'm an inventor and maker at heart, and 3D printing has become my main outlet that enables me to take ideas and make them real. I started out learning to model things in [tinkercad](https://tinkercad.com) and then worked my way up to being dangerous enough with [Fusion 360](https://www.autodesk.com/products/fusion-360/) to build just about any concept I could think of.
